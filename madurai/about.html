About Madurai

Madurai is one of the oldest cities in India, known as the cultural capital of Tamil Nadu. The city is centered around the magnificent Meenakshi Amman Temple and is steeped in Tamil traditions, art, and literature.

History

Madurai has been a major settlement for over two millennia. It was the capital of the Pandya dynasty and has played a vital role in South Indian history.

Culture

The city is renowned for its classical Tamil culture, temple festivals, and rich cuisine. It's also famous for Tamil Sangams (gatherings of poets and scholars).

Climate

Madurai has a hot semi-arid climate. Summers are quite warm, while winters are relatively mild, making the latter ideal for visiting.
